FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of what you want, give us a quick phone call. We’ll ask you a few questions about the nature of the case, what sort of interactivity the case will have, etc. Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

+

How long it takes to handle?

The time limit of any case is normally dictated by the client. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt to assemble it for you. The main general delay in the making of the papers and in waiting for the content of the pages to be sent to us by the client.

+

How do I associate with this Firm?

The process begins when you contact us with your requirements. We analyze your requirements and respond to you. On the basis of the further discussion, you can choose an engagement model that suits you the best. After that, we begin the process of case.

+

Can you help my current ongoing cases?

Yes. Give us your requirements and we have experienced expertise to help you give a new move that looks really wonders!

+

When do I pay?

For most cases, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the cases, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a frequent investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible. We accept checks, PayPal, and all major credit cards.
